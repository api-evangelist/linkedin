# LinkedIn

> LinkedIn is a professional networking platform whose public APIs span consumer identity (Sign In / Share / Verified on LinkedIn), B2B marketing (campaigns, audiences, conversions, analytics), talent solutions (job posting, Recruiter System Connect), learning, Sales Navigator, compliance archiving, and regulatory data portability. APIs are built on the Rest.li framework (URN identifiers, X-Restli-Protocol-Version 2.0.0) and are date-versioned via the Linkedin-Version (YYYYMM) header.

This llms.txt was generated by API Evangelist from the apis.yml catalog and captured artifacts in this repo (method: generated, 2026-06-20). Source of truth is LinkedIn's developer documentation on Microsoft Learn.

## APIs

- [LinkedIn Consumer API](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/linkedin/consumer/): Sign In, Share, and Verified on LinkedIn (OpenID Connect).
- [LinkedIn Marketing API](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/linkedin/marketing/): B2B advertising - campaigns, audiences, conversions, reporting, lead gen.
- [LinkedIn Learning Solutions](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/linkedin/learning/): learner activity reports and content.
- [LinkedIn Talent Solutions](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/linkedin/talent/): job posting and Recruiter System Connect.
- [LinkedIn Compliance Solutions](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/linkedin/compliance/): message archiving for regulated industries.
- [LinkedIn Sales Navigator API](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/linkedin/sales/): CRM sync, display, and sales analytics.
- [LinkedIn Regulatory API](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/linkedin/dma/): data portability and advertiser transparency (Digital Markets Act).

## Auth & conventions

- [Authentication](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/linkedin/shared/authentication/authentication): OAuth 2.0 (authorization code + client credentials); Sign In with LinkedIn is OIDC.
- OIDC discovery: https://www.linkedin.com/oauth/.well-known/openid-configuration (scopes: openid, profile, email)
- [Versioning](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/linkedin/marketing/versioning): Linkedin-Version header, YYYYMM, min 1-year support.
- [Pagination](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/linkedin/shared/api-guide/concepts/pagination): start/count (default count 10), paging object.
- [Rate limits](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/linkedin/shared/api-guide/concepts/rate-limits): per-app and per-member throttles (HTTP 429).
